How to play:

Press the "Select Player" button to change the names of player 1 and player 2. Press the "New Game" button to reset the scores. Rules: Each player starts the game by pressing the "Roll" button to roll a dice. The value of the rolled dice will be added to the current score. By pressing the "Hold" button, you add your current score to the total score. Whenever the "Hold" button is pressed it's the next player's turn to play.
